Features in KUSOX

See what makes KUSOX the best one.

1. Introduction – KUSOX System

KUSOX is a modern and responsive Point of Sale system designed to work smoothly on both mobile and desktop devices. It adapts perfectly to different screen sizes, ensuring a comfortable experience everywhere. The system offers both light and dark (night) modes to match user preferences and reduce eye strain. KUSSA is built to simplify daily business operations and improve speed at the counter. Its clean interface makes it easy for both managers and cashiers to use. The system focuses on performance, clarity, and reliability. With KUSSA, managing sales becomes more efficient and professional.

2. Smart Dashboard

The dashboard provides a clear and powerful overview of your business activity. It displays statistics and visual graphs that help you understand sales performance quickly. Data can be filtered by cashier, whether one specific cashier or all of them together. You can also analyze results by time period such as today, the last 7 days, or other ranges. This helps managers make better decisions based on real data. The dashboard updates dynamically to reflect current activity. It is the control center of the entire system.

3. Product Management

The product page allows managers to fully control the product catalog. Products can be added quickly with all necessary details. Existing products can be updated at any time to change prices or information. If a product is no longer available, it can be blocked without deleting it. This ensures clean and organized product management. The page is designed to be fast and simple to use. It helps keep stock and sales accurate.

4. Sales and Stock Management

This section is dedicated to selling products and managing stock levels. Cashiers can sell products easily with a smooth and fast process. Managers can also add products to stock when new items arrive. Every action is recorded to keep inventory up to date. This reduces errors and avoids missing or extra stock. The system ensures that sales and stock are always synchronized. It is essential for daily business operations.

5. Ticket Management

The ticket page records every sale made to clients. When a cashier sells products, they are saved automatically into a ticket. Each ticket shows the list of purchased products clearly. Tickets can be viewed later for verification or reporting. The system allows filtering tickets to find specific sales quickly. This improves transparency and control over transactions. It also helps with customer support and accounting.

6. Cashier Management

The cashier page allows managers to manage cashier accounts easily. New cashiers can be added to the system in just a few steps. Existing cashier information can be updated when needed. If a cashier is no longer active, the account can be blocked securely. This ensures controlled access to the system. Each cashier’s activity can be tracked through the system. It helps maintain security and accountability.

7. Multilanguage & RTL Support

The application supports multiple languages to provide a better experience for users from different regions. Users can easily switch between languages ​​according to their needs. The system is fully compatible with Right-To-Left (RTL) languages like Arabic, ensuring that the interface remains clear and properly aligned. This makes the app accessible, user-friendly, and adaptable to a wider audience.

8. Ticket Customization

Users can fully customize their tickets to match their business needs. Additional details can be added to provide more information for customers. The system also allows adding a logo image to give a professional and branded look to each ticket. This flexibility helps businesses create personalized and clear receipts that reflect their identity.
